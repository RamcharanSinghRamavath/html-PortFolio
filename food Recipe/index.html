Ram's Best Recipe for Best Cinnamon Rolls

Ingredients

For the dough:

  • 3/4 cup of warm milk
  • 2 teaspoons of active dry yeast
  • 1/4 cup granulated sugar
  • 1 egg plus 1 egg yolk
  • 1/4 cup butter
  • 3 cups bread flour

For the Filling

  • 2/3 cup dark brown sugar
  • 1 tablespoons ground cinnamon
  • 1/4 cup butter

Instructions

  1. Mix the Milk with the Yeast, sugar, eggs.
  2. Melt the butter and add to the mixture.
  3. Add in the flour and mix until combined into a dough
  4. Knead the dough for 10 minutes.
  5. Transfer the dough into a large bowl and cover with plastic warp.Leave it somewhere
  6. After the dough has doubled in size, roll it out into a large rectangle.
  7. Melt the butter for the filling and mix in the sugar and cinnamon.
  8. Spread the filling onto the dough thenroll the dough into a swiss roll.
  9. Cut the roll into 3cm sections and place flat into a baking tray.
  10. Preheat the oven to 350F or 180C, then brake the rolls for 20-25min until lightly brownie.
